Meet Matt.

Back 40 BBQ is a family-run catering company based right here in Marion, Iowa. Owner and pitmaster Matt Miller is always experimenting — from smoked meatloaf and BBQ burritos to smoked cream cheese and house-made sauce.

Whether it's a corporate event, a small business meeting, or a family get-together, Matt brings the smoker and the flavor. You bring the appetite.
